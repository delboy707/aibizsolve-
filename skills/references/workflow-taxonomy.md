## Workflow Selection Criteria

When selecting workflows, consider:

1. **Primary workflow**: Directly addresses the stated challenge
2. **Diagnostic workflow**: Validates assumptions before solutions
3. **Synergy workflow**: Cross-domain when problem spans functions
4. **Implementation workflow**: 30-60-90 day planning

## Source Books per Domain

Each domain draws from ~20 authoritative business books, with ~9 workflows per book, totaling ~180 workflows per domain and ~1,260 workflows across all 7 domains.
